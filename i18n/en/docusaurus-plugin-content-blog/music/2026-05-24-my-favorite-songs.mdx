My favorite singer is Jay Chou. His songs have truly been with me through my entire youth.

{/* truncate */}

Here are a few of my favorite songs of his:

## Song 1: "Blue and White Porcelain"

A few lines of the lyrics:

> Sky blue waiting for misty rain, while I wait for you
> Cooking smoke rises and curls, a thousand miles across the river
> At the base of the vase, clerical script imitates the elegance of dynasties past
> As if it were all foreshadowing for the moment I'd meet you

---

## Song 2: "Peninsula Iron Box"

A few lines of the lyrics:

> The hallway light goes off, schoolbag set down
> I walk to the window and look out from my room
> Thinking back on the book I just bought
> One called the Peninsula Iron Box
> Stacked up by the side of my bed
> Page one, page six, page seven's preface
> I never could have guessed
> That the you who read this book with me would leave

## Song 3: "Waiting for You After Class"

> In the alley where you live, I rented an apartment
> Just hoping to run into you by chance
> Three years of high school, why
> Why didn't I study harder
> I never got into the same university as you

These are songs I've kept listening to all this time. I never expected they'd hold up so well — I've been listening to them since elementary school, and I still never get tired of them.
