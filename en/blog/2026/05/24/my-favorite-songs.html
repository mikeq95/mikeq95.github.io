My Favorite Songs (Jay Chou)

May 24, 2026 · 2 min read

Jay Chou's classic songs have accompanied me throughout my youth. Here are some of my favorites:

  1. Qi Li Xiang (Seven-Mile Fragrance) - A timeless melody with the taste of summer.
  2. Ye Qu (Nocturne) - The moment that intro plays, memories come flooding back.
  3. Qing Tian (Clear Sky) - The secret crush and regret of adolescence.
  4. Yi Fu Zhi Ming (In the Name of the Father) - The pinnacle of his dark style.
  5. Qing Hua Ci (Blue and White Porcelain) - A beautiful ode to Chinese aesthetics.

These songs are more than just melodies — they are the mark of time.
